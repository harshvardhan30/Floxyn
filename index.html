FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How long does it take to build an automation?
+

Simple scripts (Excel automation, PDF processing) take 3–7 days. Medium projects (API integrations, dashboards) take 1–2 weeks. Complex ML pipelines take 2–4 weeks. We always give a clear timeline before starting.

Do I need technical knowledge to use the automation?
+

No technical knowledge needed at all. We build automation that fits into your existing workflow — whether that's Excel, a web dashboard, or WhatsApp. We also provide full training and documentation so your team can use it confidently.

Is my data safe with you?
+

Absolutely. We sign NDAs for all projects and build automations that run on your own systems or servers. Your data never leaves your environment unless you specifically want cloud processing. Security is built-in by default.

What if something breaks after delivery?
+

Every project includes 30 days of free support after delivery. If anything breaks or behaves unexpectedly, we fix it at no extra cost. After 30 days, we offer affordable maintenance retainer plans.

Can you automate processes in our existing software (Tally, SAP, Zoho)?
+

Yes! We have experience integrating with Tally, Zoho, Salesforce, SAP, and most popular Indian business software. We use APIs, RPA techniques, or direct database connections depending on what's available.

How do we get started?
+

Simply fill the contact form below or WhatsApp us. We'll schedule a free 30-minute discovery call, understand your workflow, and send you a free audit with a clear proposal — no commitment required.
