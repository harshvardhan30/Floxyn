Questions

Frequently Asked

How long does a typical automation project take?
+

Most projects are delivered in 1–3 weeks from kickoff. A single automation (e.g. invoice extraction or auto-reporting) takes 5–10 days. Complex multi-model ML pipelines take 2–4 weeks. We timeline everything in writing before starting.

Do you work with international clients?
+

Absolutely. We actively serve clients across India, UAE, Singapore, UK, USA, Canada, and Australia. All work is remote, all code is cloud-deployable, and we handle multi-currency and multi-timezone requirements by default.

What ML frameworks and tools do you use?
+

We use Python as our primary language, with PyTorch and scikit-learn for ML, HuggingFace for NLP, FastAPI for APIs, Airflow for orchestration, MLflow for experiment tracking, and Docker/AWS for deployment. We choose the best tool for each problem, not the most fashionable one.

How do you ensure data security?
+

Every engagement starts with an NDA. We deploy inside your infrastructure whenever possible. For cloud deployments, we use isolated environments with strict IAM policies. We never use client data for training models for other clients.

What's your pricing model?
+

We work on fixed-scope project pricing (most common) and monthly retainer models for ongoing automation and model maintenance. Pricing is transparent and agreed upfront — no hourly billing surprises. Contact us for a custom quote.
