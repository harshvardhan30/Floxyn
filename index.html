Who We Are

About Auto Solution

A remote-first automation studio on a simple mission: give people back the hours they lose to repetitive, manual work.

Auto Solution started when our founders kept watching skilled teams burn entire days on copy-paste reporting, manual reconciliation, and Excel busywork — tasks a script could finish in seconds. So we built one. Then another. Then a studio around it.

Today we design and ship Python, Excel, OCR and ML-powered automation for businesses across India, the Middle East, Singapore and beyond — small, senior, remote-first team, no bloated process, no bench-warming.

We measure ourselves the same way our clients do: hours saved, errors removed, and how fast a project goes from "manual mess" to "it just runs now."

Common Questions

How long does it take to build an automation?
+

Simple scripts take 3–7 days. Medium projects (integrations, dashboards) take 1–2 weeks. Complex ML pipelines take 2–4 weeks. We always give a clear timeline upfront.

Do you work with clients outside India?
+

Yes! We work with clients globally — UAE, Singapore, UK, USA and more. Remote-first company serving businesses worldwide.

Do I need technical knowledge?
+

Zero technical knowledge needed. We build automation that fits your existing workflow — Excel, web dashboard, or WhatsApp. Full training and documentation included.

Is my data safe?
+

Absolutely. We sign NDAs for all projects. Automation runs on your own systems. Your data never leaves your environment without explicit consent.

Can you integrate with Tally, SAP, Zoho, Salesforce?
+

Yes! We integrate with Tally, Zoho, Salesforce, SAP, QuickBooks, and most popular software via APIs, RPA, or direct database connections.
