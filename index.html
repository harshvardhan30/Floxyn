FAQ

Common Questions

How long does it take to build an automation?
+

Simple scripts take 3–7 days. Medium projects (API integrations, dashboards) take 1–2 weeks. Complex ML pipelines take 2–4 weeks. We always give a clear timeline upfront.

Do you work with international clients?
+

Absolutely! We work with clients across USA, UK, UAE, Germany, Singapore, Australia, and more. We're fully remote-first and work across all time zones with async and real-time collaboration.

Is my data safe with you?
+

We sign NDAs for all projects and build automations that run on your own systems or servers. Your data never leaves your environment unless you specifically want cloud processing.

What if something breaks after delivery?
+

Every project includes 30 days of free support after delivery. If anything breaks or behaves unexpectedly, we fix it at no extra cost. After that, affordable retainer plans are available.

Can you integrate with our existing software?
+

Yes! We've integrated with Tally, Zoho, Salesforce, SAP, HubSpot, Shopify, Amazon, and most popular business software via APIs, RPA techniques, or direct database connections.
